Biography

Hi, I am a PostDoc researcher at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, mentored by Prof. Zhijian Jin. Previously, I obtained my B.E. and Ph.D. from EE, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I was on a research stay at operation research group, Technical University of Denmark, supervised by Prof. Pierre Pinson in 2021.

My research interests include predictive and prescriptive analytics especially machine learning applications for power systems, energy forecasting, electricity markets, and decision making under uncertainty. Currently, I am focusing on the interplay between forecasting and decision making.
